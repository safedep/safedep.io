Announcing DefectDojo Integration with [vet](https://github.com/safedep/vet) allowing users to export OSS dependency scan results to DefectDojo. Continue leveraging [DefectDojo](https://defectdojo.com/) for your vulnerability management needs while using [vet](https://github.com/safedep/vet) for identifying vulnerable and malicious open source packages, staying safe and compliant.

## TL;DR

Use [vet](https://github.com/safedep/vet) to identify vulnerable and malicious open source packages in your project and export the scan results to DefectDojo for vulnerability management.
