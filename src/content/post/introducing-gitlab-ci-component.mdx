Announcing the new GitLab CI/CD Component, available in GitLab CI Catalog for seamless integration of [vet](https://github.com/safedep/vet) in GitLab CI. Protect against vulnerable and malicious packages in your GitLab projects. This integration enables GitLab users to easily integrate [vet](https://github.com/safedep/vet) with their GitLab CI pipelines. This integration protects GitLab projects from vulnerable and malicious packages in the dependency tree.
